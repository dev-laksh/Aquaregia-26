The Prophecy

Wizards, Witches, and Muggles of Jaipur! The time has come to raise your wands. University Maharaja College invites you to the most magical gathering of the year. Prepare for a night where science meets sorcery, and logic meets legend.

The Alchemy of Aquaregia

HNO₃ + 3 HCl

"The Royal Water"

In the world of science, Aqua Regia is a potent mixture, the only substance capable of dissolving gold—the noblest of metals.

In our world, Aquaregia 2026 is the crucible. It is a mixture of Art, Technology, Music, and Intellect. It dissolves the barriers between departments and refines the raw talent of every student into pure gold.

Just as the potion requires precision, our fest requires passion. UMC's historic grounds will transform into a laboratory of dreams where memories are brewed.
